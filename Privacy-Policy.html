🔐 1. No Personal Data Collection

AlPlayHub operates on a strict no-personal-data-collection policy. We believe in providing an anonymous and secure gaming experience.

We do not ask for account registration or login credentials
We do not request or store names, emails, phone numbers, or passwords
We do not collect IP addresses, location data, or device information
We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics
We do not create user profiles or behavioral tracking

Result: Complete anonymity while gaming and browsing our platform!

💾 2. Your Data Stays With You

Any content you interact with is saved locally in your browser using secure client-side storage mechanisms like localStorage and sessionStorage.

Local browser storage only
Only you can view your data
Never reaches our servers
Clears when you clear browser cache

What's Stored Locally:

  • Your uploaded game drafts and submissions
  • Theme preferences and display settings
  • Recently played games list
  • User profile information (if provided voluntarily)
  • App preferences and customizations

Important: Data will reset if you clear your browser cache, switch devices, or use incognito mode.

🤝 7. Third-Party Services

AlPlayHub uses minimal third-party services, all chosen for their privacy-respecting practices.

CDN Services:

We use content delivery networks (CDNs) for loading external libraries like Tailwind CSS and Font Awesome. These services may log basic request information but do not track users across sites.

Firebase Hosting:

Our platform is hosted on Firebase, which may collect basic server logs for security and performance purposes. No personal user data is involved in this process.

We carefully vet all third-party services to ensure they align with our privacy-first principles.
